About EEYP

The Erasmian European Youth Parliament brings together students to defend liberal democracy

Committees

Defending Democracy Through Debate

Six committees tackling the most pressing challenges to liberal democracy in Europe

AFCO

Constitutional Affairs

Topic: How should the EU reform its treaties to better protect liberal democratic principles without risking over-constitutionalisation?

Explore the balance between safeguarding democracy, strengthening EU institutions, and preserving member state sovereignty.

CULT

Education and Social Affairs

Topic: How can the EU strengthen education and social welfare as foundations of democracy?

Explore how inclusive education, social security, and equal opportunities can foster democratic participation and counter anti-liberal movements.

INTA

International Trade

Topic: Can and should European trade policy promote liberal democracy worldwide?

Explore how democracy and human rights clauses in trade agreements can shape global governance, balance economic interests, and advance European values abroad.

AFET

Foreign Affairs

Topic: How can the EU effectively defend democracy and human rights at its external borders?

Explore the EU's response to authoritarian regimes, the role of sanctions, and the challenges of maintaining democratic values in its neighbourhood and foreign policy.

ENVI

Environment and Public Health

Topic: How can the EU fight climate change through democratic means?

Explore how environmental protection, public participation, and democratic decision-making can align to create fair and effective climate policies across Europe.

LIBE

Civil Liberties & Justice

Topic: How can the EU safeguard freedom of expression and the press in the digital age?

Explore how the EU can address disinformation, protect data privacy, and uphold digital rights while maintaining an open and democratic online space.
